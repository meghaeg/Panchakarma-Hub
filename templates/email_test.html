Instructions: Enter your email address to test the email service.
Enter the email address where you want to receive the test email.
Setup Instructions
  1. Enable 2-Factor Authentication on your Gmail account
  2. Generate App Password:
    • Go to Google Account Security
    • Click "2-Step Verification"
    • Click "App passwords"
    • Select "Mail" and generate password
  3. Update Configuration:
    • Open email_config.py
    • Replace YOUR_APP_PASSWORD_HERE with your App Password
    • Set testing_mode: False
Security Note: Never share your App Password. It's only for this application.
