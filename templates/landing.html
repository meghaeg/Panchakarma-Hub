Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Panchakarma therapy

Panchakarma is a comprehensive detoxification and rejuvenation program from Ayurveda. It consists of five therapeutic procedures designed to cleanse the body of toxins and restore natural balance.

A complete Panchakarma program typically lasts 14-21 days, though shorter programs of 7-10 days are also available depending on individual needs and health conditions.

Most healthy adults can benefit from Panchakarma. However, it's not recommended for pregnant women, children under 12, or people with certain medical conditions. A consultation with an Ayurvedic physician is essential.

The treatment includes preparatory procedures, main cleansing therapies, and rejuvenation phase. You'll receive personalized diet plans, herbal medicines, and lifestyle guidance throughout the program.

You can book appointments through our portal by selecting your preferred center, choosing available dates, and completing the registration process. You'll need to provide your Aadhar number and health information.
