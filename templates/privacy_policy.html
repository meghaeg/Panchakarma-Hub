Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last updated: {{ current_date or "January 2024" }}

Information We Collect

Personal Information:
  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Aadhar number (for patient verification)
  • Date of birth and age
  • Address and location details
Health Information:
  • Medical history and health conditions
  • Current medications and treatments
  • Vital signs and progress records
  • Treatment feedback and reviews
Professional Information:
  • Medical licenses and certifications
  • Professional qualifications and experience
  • Centre accreditation details

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our Panchakarma therapy services
  • To match patients with qualified doctors and centres
  • To track treatment progress and outcomes
  • To send appointment confirmations and reminders
  • To ensure quality standards and regulatory compliance
  • To generate anonymized reports for healthcare research

Data Protection

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed
  • We comply with applicable healthcare data protection laws
  • Patient data is never shared without explicit consent

Information Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your assigned healthcare providers for treatment purposes
  • With regulatory authorities as required by law
  • With your explicit consent for research or quality improvement
  • In case of medical emergencies to ensure your safety

Your Rights

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Receive a copy of your data in portable format

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login preferences
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns for service improvement

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
